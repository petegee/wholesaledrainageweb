Sorry, as we have split up there will be no new news or gigs!

2nd March 1997

Wholesale Drainage appear on the Massey University Student's Association "MUSAK" CD compilation being distributed to first-year students. The CD features 16 bands from around Palmerston North, each contributing one track. Wholesale contributed a live version of 'Malice' recorded at our 'live to air' on Radio Massey in August 1997. Limited numbers will be available at local retailers and from The Celibate Monk.

12th December 1997

'Celibate Monks' are proud to present the "Intemperance" Compilation CD. Bands featured include Wholesale Drainage, Master Cheese Maker, Fader, and more. The CD features a range of styles from Industrial, Hardcore, Goth, Punk, Experimental, to Pop. All bands are from Palmerston North, New Zealand. The price is $6 (NZ). Check out the Celibate Monks web page for more details.

13th November 1997

Dave Drainage has finished his 'Celibate Monks' web page and is now online. See related stuff at the bottom of this page for information on the 'Celibate Monks' recording collective and the compilation albums he's putting together.

30th August 1997

To play the samples on the download page, you MUST have an MP3 player. The best one is Winamp, which is free and easy to use. MP3 is a format for encoding digital music at CD quality in stereo, 11 times smaller than standard WAV format.

Related Stuff

Dave Drainage runs the Celibate Monks recording artist's collective. This is not a record label but an organization to help promote local music in a cheap, non-profit way. The first CD, "Excommunicated," features 11 local bands, including Wholesale Drainage, and is available for NZ $6.00. Dave is now working on the follow-up CD, "Intemperance."

Dave White from Nelson runs 'Yellow Bike' records and has been releasing 'Valve' CD compilations for years. Wholesale Drainage features on 'Valve 9'. Contact Dave White at valve@Nelson.PlaNet.org.nz for more information.
